Aid Tables

Since 2006 the EFA Global Monitoring Report has included tables on development assistance and aid. The focus is on Official Development Assistance (ODA) - that is, public funds provided to developing countries to promote their economic and social development.

GMR 2015

Aid disbursements tables 2015 [XLS] which contain:

  • Table 1: Bilateral and multilateral ODA - per donor
  • Table 2: Bilateral and multilateral aid to education - per donor
  • Table 3: Aid to education – per recipient
  • Table 4: ODA recipients (table not in printed report)
