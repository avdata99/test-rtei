Convention On The Right To Education And The First Experience Of Higher Education In Chile In A Context Of Imprisonment In Chile

July 14, 2016

In Chile in 2016, two initiatives on the right to education in prison are being put forth. The first action is the convention to be held August 18-19, with the goal of increasing right to education in prisons, analyze the initiatives put forth by the Ministries of Justice and Education, and know the various organizations that organize initiatives inside prisons that support social reintegration.
