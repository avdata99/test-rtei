National Policy on Education

The National Policy on Education (NPE) is the national guideline for the effective administration, management and implementation of education at all tiers of government.

The National Policy on Education therefore, is a statement of intentions, expectations, goals, prescriptions, standards and requirements for quality education delivery in Nigeria. Nigeria like most other countries of the world is undergoing rapid social, economic and political reforms.
