Zonal Screening Exercise scheduled from 12 June 2016 to 25 June 2016. Selected applicants have been sent an email confirmation and should bring the following to the centre.

  1. Pre-Screening Examination Result Notice (Log in to download)
  2. Primary School Testimonial (Original)
  3. Primary School Certificate (Original)
  4. Secondary School Testimonial (Original)
  5. Statement of Results for WAEC/NECO or equivalent (Original/Computer Printout)
  6. Trade Test Certificate (for tradesmen/tradeswomen only)
  7. Certificate of State of Origin
  8. Birth Certificate/Declaration of Age
  9. Completed and signed 75RRI form (Certificate of Guarantor with passport photograph)
  10. Sports wears (blue short, white vest and canvas)
  11. Writing materials

Applicant Eligibility

  • Applicants shall be of Nigerian origin by birth.
  • All Applicants must attend zonal screening exercise at the designated zonal centres for their respective states.
  • Non-Tradesmen & Women Applicants must be aged between 18 - 22 years by June 1, 2016.
  • Tradesmen & Women Applicants must be aged between 18 - 26 years by June 1, 2016.
  • Applicants must be medically, physically, and psychologically fit and must not be less than 1.65 meters tall for male and 1.56 meters for female.
  • Applicants must be free of any Criminal conviction.
  • Applicants must print their completed online forms, attest it and submit it at the screening centres during the screening exercise period.
